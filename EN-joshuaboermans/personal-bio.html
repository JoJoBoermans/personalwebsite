My Story

From IT Sales to IT Development - A Journey of Growth and Discovery

Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Joshua Boermans, a 29-year-old IT enthusiast from Hoofddorp currently undergoing an exciting career transition. After more than six years of experience in IT sales at both startups and established companies, I've decided to take my passion for technology a step further and transition into IT development.

What started as curiosity about the technical side of the products I was selling has now grown into a genuine passion for programming, systems administration, and IT innovation. I strongly believe in lifelong learning and am currently busy further developing my technical skills to successfully tackle this new challenge.

My Career Journey

IT Sales Experience (2019-2025)

Started at XcellerateLabs where I worked with cloud-native startups, followed by roles at Trusted Shops (e-commerce security) and NCC Group (software escrow services). As Head of Growth at UNIT, I gained hands-on experience with low-code development and business automation.

IT Bootcamp (2025)

Currently pursuing an intensive IT bootcamp at BEE-Organisation where I specialize in front-end and back-end development, virtual machines, and network management. It's here that I discovered my strong affinity for software development and network administration.
